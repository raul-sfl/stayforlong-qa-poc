# Lanzar búsqueda con niño HOME (Desktop)

## Preconditions
- Browser is open and ready

## Steps
1. Navigate to https://www.stayforlong.com/?market=us&lang=en
2. Type 'BARCELONA' in destination field
3. Select Barcelona from search results
4. Click room and guest settings button
5. Click the + button to add 1 child
6. Click the age selector button to open the age dropdown
7. Select age 6 from the age list
8. Click the Save button
9. Click the Search button
10. Wait until search results are loaded
